User Guide

Download Guide Privacy

The Philosophy. Gleano has no folders, no tasks, and no checkboxes. It is purely person-centric. You don't file data by project; you file it by person.

1 Capturing Data (The Magic)

Gleano relies on intercepting your device's clipboard. This bypasses WhatsApp's strict share restrictions.

  • Highlight any promise, recommendation, or meeting in a chat.
  • Tap Copy.
  • Switch to Gleano. The Assign sheet will slide up instantly.
  • Manual Entry: Tap the + button in the bottom right corner to type an entry yourself without copying from a chat.

2 The Assign Sheet & Smart NLP

  • Text Box: Shows what you copied. If you used the + button, this becomes a text box to type manually.
  • The Intent Chip: Gleano guesses if you owe them or they owe you. Tap to flip between "I OWE", "OWES ME", and "FROM THEM".
  • Assign To: Type a name. Your most contacted people appear as quick-tap pills.
  • GLEAN: Saves the entry and clears your clipboard.

3 Zero-Tap Due Dates (The NLP Engine)

You don't have to tap the calendar manually. Gleano reads the text.

📌 Copy "Meeting tomorrow at 3pm" → Gleano automatically sets tomorrow's date and 3:00 PM.

📌 Copy "Seminar on 30th next month" → Gleano automatically calculates May 30th.

  • The "Tap to Confirm" Badge: When Gleano auto-detects a time but has to guess the date (e.g., it picks "today" or "tomorrow"), a subtle pulsing badge appears on the date button saying "tap to confirm". If it guessed right, just hit GLEAN. If you want to change it, tap the badge to open the calendar.
  • The Rule: You can save a time without a date (it defaults to today/tomorrow). You can save a date without a time (defaults to 12:00 AM).

4 Viewing Data (The Dossier)

Tap any person on the Home Screen to open their profile. Data is automatically split into three sections: I OWE, OWES ME, and FROM THEM.

5 Marking as Done

Inside a person's profile, swipe left on any entry. The background will flash dark crimson, and the item is permanently deleted. If a person has no remaining entries, they are automatically removed from the Home Screen.

6 Deep Search

Forgot who recommended that plumber? Don't search for "Mike". Search for "plumber". The search engine scans the actual text of every debt across all your contacts.

⚡ Tips for Power Users

  • Smart Sorting: The more you interact with someone, the higher their name appears on the quick-assign list.
  • The Share Sheet: Apps like Chrome, Telegram, and Apple Notes allow you to highlight text and hit "Share → Gleano". This triggers the exact same sheet.
  • Zero Network Required: Gleano does not use the internet. It works perfectly on Airplane Mode.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Gleano is a zero-friction personal CRM designed to help you manage promises, recommendations, and social debts. We are committed to absolute privacy. Gleano does not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on any server — there is no Gleano server.

1. Data Collection & Storage
Gleano operates 100% locally on your device. When you copy or share text into Gleano, it is parsed on-device for intent (e.g., "I owe" vs "owes me") and saved into a local database on your phone, along with the contact name and any due date you assign. Notifications are scheduled and shown by Android's local notification system. None of this data is transmitted off your device. We do not have, and cannot obtain, a copy of your data.

2. No Servers, No Tracking
Gleano does not use analytics, crash reporters, tracking pixels, advertising networks, or any third-party SDK that phones home. The release build of the app does not even request the Android INTERNET permission, so it is technically incapable of making a network request. We do not have access to your contacts, location, microphone, or camera.

3. Data Deletion
If you uninstall Gleano, all associated data is permanently and immediately deleted by the operating system. You can also delete individual entries inside the app at any time by swiping left on any glean.

4. Permissions
Gleano asks for a small set of Android permissions, all used strictly on-device:

  • Notifications — to display reminders for the gleans you save.
  • Alarms & reminders (exact alarms) — to make those reminders fire at the time you set, even when your phone is in Doze mode or battery saver.
  • Run on boot — so your scheduled reminders survive a phone restart and don't get silently dropped.

Gleano does not request internet access, contacts, location, microphone, camera, SMS, or external storage. You can review and revoke any granted permission at any time from your phone's system settings; the in-app Settings panel includes a "Test notification" button so you can verify the reminder pipeline yourself.

5. Children's Privacy
Because Gleano collects no data and contacts no servers, we have nothing to share with anyone — including data on children. The app is suitable for general audiences.

6. Changes to this Policy
If we ever change this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above and post the new version at this URL before shipping any change in the app. We will not retroactively change the privacy posture of a build that has already been released.
